Designers don‚Äôt know how component and style updates affect their files.

Two main characteristics contribute to the reluctance to update:

Lack of control

A component update affects all of its instances in a file.
Similarly, a style update affects all of its applications.

Lack of clarity

It is unclear if the update will break designs.

In other words: designers don't know how updates affect their files, so they usually ignore notifications to update.

The often ignored notification

Putting off new updates results in outdated files.

There is an opportunity to encourage designers to update their components!

We want designers to review and accept changes!

How might we give designers more control & clarity so they can update components and styles confidently?

By creating a more granular updating experience.
